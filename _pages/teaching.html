--- layout: archive title: "Teaching" permalink: /teaching/ author_profile: true --- ## Teaching - **Fundamentals of Computer Science** Undergraduate course, University of St. Gallen (HSG), Bachelor in International Affairs (BIA), 2025 This course provides a foundational introduction to computer science for bachelor students in International Affairs. It covers essential theory and practical skills for algorithmic problem solving and modern programming. - **Embedded Sensing Systems** Undergraduate course, University of St. Gallen (HSG), Bachelor in Computer Science (BCS), 2025 This elective course provides an in-depth introduction to embedded sensing systems, bridging theory and hands-on practice. Students design, program, and prototype systems capable of real-world data sensing and communication. - **Computer Networks** Undergraduate course, University of St. Gallen (HSG), Bachelor in Computer Science (BCS), 2025 This course is co-taught by Simon and me. We guide students through the essential concepts and practices of computer networking. - **Network and IoT Security** Undergraduate course, Ostschweizer Fachhochschule (OST), Department of Computer Science, 2023 Lecturer for the module “Network and IoT Security” (4 ECTS), introducing bachelor students to networking principles and IoT security challenges. - **Computer Network Security Principles** Graduate course, University of Zürich, Communication Systems Group, 2022 Lecturer for the MSc course “Computer Network Security Principles,” teaching master’s students advanced concepts in network security.
